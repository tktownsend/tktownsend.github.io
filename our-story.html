Troy Townsend, PhD – COO

After 12 years as Professor of Chemistry & Materials Science at St. Mary’s College of Maryland, Troy Townsend retired to focus full‑time on applied research and technology transition. His work in electrochemistry and materials innovation led to multiple patents and peer‑reviewed publications, including the Materials Advances article on electrostamping copper, nickel, tin, zinc, and brass.
