Jason P. Shaw, USN (Ret.) – CEO

Jason is a retired Aviation Maintenance Duty Officer (AMDO) with 28 years of experience in aero maintenance, logistics, and sustainment. His background spans NAVAIR, NAVSEA, and Pentagon roles, with deep expertise in program management, cost, schedule, performance, acquisition consulting, and risk management.

Troy K. Townsend, PhD – COO

Troy is a retired Professor of Chemistry & Materials Science from St. Mary’s College of Maryland, completing 12 years of teaching and research in electrochemistry, inorganic chemistry, and materials science. His work focuses on electroplating, corrosion prevention, and advanced coatings for mission readiness.

Embedded Highlight: Research-Driven Readiness

Shaw & Townsend Innovations bridges academic research and operational sustainment. Our patented glowing composite coatings and electrostamping methods are not standalone lab curiosities—they are designed to survive NAWCAD engineering tests and real-world fleet conditions.

By combining laboratory rigor with decades of U.S. Navy maintenance experience, we focus on solutions that can be implemented, maintained, and trusted in the field.
